1. Gulab Jamun



Ingredients


  • 1 cup dry milk powder
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter), melted
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ cup warm milk
  • 1 pinch ground cardamom
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for deep frying
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 7 fluid ounces water
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • 1 pinch ground cardamom

Recipe


  1. Make the dough
  2. Form balls
  3. Fry the Gulab Jamun
  4. Make the sugar syrup
  5. Soak the Gulab Jamun
  6. Serve & enjoy!

2. Rasgulla



Ingredients


  • 2 litre milk
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoon lime juice
  • 3 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom

Recipe


  1. Prepare the chhena
  2. Strain the chhena from whey
  3. Strain the whey
  4. Allow the water to separate
  5. Prepare sugar syrup
  6. Knead the chhena
  7. Boil the Rasgullas
  8. Cook covered
  9. Enjoy!

3. Barfi



Ingredients


  • 2½ cup milk powder
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup ghee / clarified butter
  • 3 tbsp pistachios
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Recipe


  1. Dissolve 1 cup sugar in 1/2 cup water. Boil until it forms a string consistency
  2. Slowly stir in 2 cups of milk powder
  3. Mix in 1/4 cup ghee and cook until the mixture thickens
  4. Add 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  5. Pour into a greased tray, garnish with nuts, let it cool, and cut into pieces

3. Jelbi



Ingredients


  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 Water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ¼ cup ghee / oil

Recipe


  1. Mix 1 cup flour, a little water, and food color to make a thick batter. Rest for 1 hour
  2. Boil 1 cup sugar with 1/2 cup water until slightly sticky
  3. Pour batter into a bottle or bag, squeeze spirals into hot oil, and fry until crispy
  4. Dip fried jalebis in warm sugar syrup for a few seconds

3. Kaju Katli



Ingredients


  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Silver leaf

Recipe


  1. Blend 1 cup cashews into a fine powder
  2. Boil 1/2 cup sugar with 1/4 cup water until dissolved
  3. Add cashew powder to the syrup and stir on low heat until it forms a dough
  4. Roll the dough between parchment sheets and cut into diamond shapes
